Information about the Venues

Image by Roman Catholic Diocese of Kamloops

The ceremony will be held at St. Jude's Catholic Church in 100 Mile House. St. Jude's serves the 100 Mile community, as well as the communities of Lac la Hache, Bridge Lake, and Canime Lake. Mass and Service times are located on their website.

Image by Booking.com

The reception will be held at Spruce Hill Resort & Spa in 108 Mile House. The Hills is a popular wellness resort and it offers many activities for guests to enjoy, such as showshoeing, skiing, horseback riding, hiking, mountain biking, and canoeing. There are two restaurants on-site and three lounges.
